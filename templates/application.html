Research Proposal Application

If you would like to propose a project to evaluate variants you have identified in the TGAC browser, please complete the form below. Please note that if you have a clinical protocol it is expected that research conducted with TGAC participants will be performed under that clinical protocol. For basic science researchers who do not have a clinical protocol, the TGAC team may be able to assist with basic phenotyping or help the investigator find an applicable clinical protocol.

Please provide as much detail as necessary to convey the relevant information for your proposed project. The TGAC team will contact you for further discussion once the application is received.
